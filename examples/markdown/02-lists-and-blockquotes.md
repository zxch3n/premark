# Lists and Blockquotes

## Unordered lists

- Root bullet with **bold** text
- Another bullet with `inline code`
  - Nested bullet with _emphasis_
  - Nested bullet with a [link](https://example.com)
- Third bullet with ~~strikethrough~~

## Ordered lists

1. Step one opens the process
2. Step two includes a longer explanation that should wrap onto the next line when the canvas width gets tighter
3. Step three contains nested items
   1. Nested ordered item
   2. Another nested ordered item

> Blockquotes should indent visually and keep inline formatting like **strong**, _emphasis_, and `code`.
>
> > Nested blockquotes should also work, especially when the sentence becomes long enough to wrap across multiple lines inside the quote gutter.

Regular paragraph after the quote to verify spacing resets correctly.
